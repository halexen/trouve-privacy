Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Core principle

Your documents stay on your device.

Original files (PDFs, images) are never uploaded to our servers.

Some features require processing extracted text through a secure AI service (Anthropic — Claude) to enable search, categorization, and analysis.

Data processed

  • Extracted text — temporarily sent to Claude for indexing and search
  • Documents — stored locally on your device only
  • Anonymous device ID — used to secure API requests
  • Subscription data — managed by Apple / Google / RevenueCat

Storage

We do not store any documents or user content on our servers. All data remains on your device.

Third-party services

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — text processing
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Apple / Google — payments and distribution
