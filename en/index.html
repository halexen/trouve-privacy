🔒
Core principle

Trouvé is designed to respect your privacy. Your original documents (PDFs, images) remain stored locally on your device. Original files are not sent to our servers.

✓ Your files stay on your device — some features may use extracted text to enable search, indexing, and analysis.
📄
Data collected

Trouvé only processes the data necessary for the app to function:

  • Document content — text extracted from your PDFs and scanned images may be sent to the Claude API (Anthropic) for indexing, search, categorization, and certain intelligent features. Original files (PDFs, images) are never transmitted.
  • Voice audio — when using voice search, your voice is processed locally on your device.
  • Device identifier — an anonymous identifier is used to authenticate your API requests. It is not linked to your identity.
  • Subscription data — managed by RevenueCat and Apple/Google under their own policies.
📷
Permissions used
  • Camera — only to scan paper documents
  • Microphone — only for voice search
  • Files / Photos — to import your PDFs and images
  • Notifications — for smart reminders (optional)

No permission is used for any other purpose.

🔔
Smart reminders

Trouvé can analyze the content of your documents to detect important dates (appointments, deadlines, bills) and send you reminders. Notifications are generated on your device. Depending on the feature used, extracted text may be processed to identify useful information.

🤝
Third-party services
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processing extracted text for indexing, search, and intelligent features
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Apple / Google — distribution and payments

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers. We do not store copies of your original documents on our servers.

💾
Backup and deletion

Your documents are stored locally on your device. Automatic backup via iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android) may be enabled depending on your system settings. If you uninstall the app, all local data is deleted.
