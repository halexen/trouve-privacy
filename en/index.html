Privacy Policy

Politique de confidentialité · Política de privacidad

Last updated: April 2026
🔒
Core Principle
Your documents remain stored locally on your device. Original files are never sent to our servers.
✓
Your PDF files and images never leave your phone
✓
Only extracted text may be processed by AI for search and analysis
✓
No advertising, no data resale
✓
Android automatic backup is disabled to protect your data
📄
Data Collected
  • Document content — text extracted from your PDFs and scanned images may be sent to the Claude API (Anthropic) for indexing, search, categorization, and certain intelligent features. Original files (PDFs, images) are never transmitted.
  • Voice audio — when using voice search, your voice is processed locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers.
  • Anonymous device identifier — used only to authenticate your API requests. Not linked to your identity.
  • Subscription data — managed by RevenueCat and Apple/Google under their own privacy policies.
📷
Permissions Used
  • Camera — only to scan paper documents
  • Microphone — only for voice search
  • Files / Photos — to import your PDFs and images
  • Notifications — for smart reminders (optional)

No permission is used for any purpose other than those described above.

🔔
Smart Reminders

Trouvé can analyze the content of your documents to detect important dates (appointments, deadlines, bills) and send you reminders. Notifications are generated on your device. Depending on the feature used, extracted text may be processed to identify useful information.

🤝
Third-Party Services
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processing extracted text for indexing, search, summaries, and AI chat. Policy: anthropic.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple / Google — distribution and payments

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers. We do not store copies of your original documents on our servers.

💾
Backup and Deletion

Your documents are stored locally on your device. Automatic backup via iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android) may be enabled depending on your system settings.

If you uninstall the app, all local data is permanently deleted.
